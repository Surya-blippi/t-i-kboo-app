# Privacy Policy — tikboo

**Effective date:** 15 June 2026

tikboo ("tikboo", "we", "us") is a mobile app that analyzes a WhatsApp chat export you choose to import and returns a playful, Wrapped-style summary plus an AI "vibe check." This policy explains what we handle and how. By using tikboo, you agree to this policy.

## 1. What we process
- **Chat content you import.** When you export a chat from WhatsApp and import or share it into tikboo, the app reads that file's contents — messages, participant names, timestamps, emoji and media markers.
- **Context you provide.** The "who are they" (gender) and "relationship type" you select for an analysis.
- **Purchase status.** Whether you hold an active subscription (handled by Apple and RevenueCat — see below). We do not receive your full payment card details.

We do **not** collect your contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers for advertising. tikboo has no account or login.

## 2. How your chat is used
- **On your device.** Parsing and the statistical breakdown (message counts, timing, emoji, streaks, etc.) happen locally on your phone.
- **Sent to our AI provider.** To generate the AI portion of your report (the vibe read, flags with quotes, roast, etc.), a **sample of messages and the aggregate statistics** are sent to our AI processor, **OpenRouter** (https://openrouter.ai), which routes the request to a third-party language model. This transmission is used solely to produce your report. We do not use your messages to train models, and we do not sell them.
- We do **not** operate servers that store your chats. The message sample is processed transiently to create your report and is not retained by us.

## 3. What is stored, and where
- **Locally on your device:** your completed reports (your "history"), your selected AI model, and (if entered) your OpenRouter API key. This data stays on your device. You can delete any report by swiping it in History, and removing all local data by uninstalling the app.
- **We keep no copy** of your chats or reports on our own infrastructure.

## 4. Third-party services
tikboo relies on these processors, each governed by its own privacy policy:
- **OpenRouter** — AI processing of your message sample. https://openrouter.ai/privacy
- **Apple** — subscription billing and app distribution. https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **RevenueCat** — subscription/entitlement management. https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

## 5. Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing. Data is shared only with the processors above, only to operate the app. When you tap "Share to Story," the image you generate is shared by you, through your device's share sheet, to the destination you choose.

## 6. Data retention & your choices
- Reports remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
- Because we store nothing server-side, there is no account for us to delete; uninstalling tikboo removes its local data from your device.
- You control what you import — only import chats you have the right to analyze.

## 7. Security
We use reputable providers and encrypted (HTTPS) connections for data in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

## 8. Children
tikboo is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country). If you are under that age, please do not use the app.

## 9. Changes
We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
